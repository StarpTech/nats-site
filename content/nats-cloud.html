NATS Cloud

NATS Cloud is a managed service based on NATS, allowing you to instantly connect your resources to NATS messaging and scale without the need to maintain or provision any hardware yourself.

FAQ

What is NATS Cloud?
NATS Cloud is a subscription-based publish-and-subscribe messaging service hosted, and managed by the NATS team. With NATS Cloud, you get a scalable and automated NATS cluster maintained by the best messaging team in the industry, without having to manage anything yourself. You’ll automatically receive the latest updates and get support as part of your subscription too.
Can I try NATS Cloud for free?
We maintain a demo server at telnet demo.nats.io 4222 but for ongoing use and access to NATS Cloud, you must sign up for a plan.
What is your Service Level Agreement
Please contact us at info@nats.io for more information about enterprise pricing and SLAs
What happens if I exceed my plans limits?
You will be notified via email that your plan limit has been exceeded, to discuss a Plan that meets your requirements.
What is a "user" and what is a "connection"?
A user is a person or a machine account that has been granted access credentials to connect to a NATS Cloud cluster.
A connection is a NATS client API connection to a NATS Cloud cluster. Our pricing plans count the number of simultaneous/concurrent connections at any instant in time.
What is the message size?
512K
How can I track my usage?
The NATS team provides a dashboard as part of NATS Cloud to monitor usage.
How secure is NATS Cloud?
Protect your cluster with authentication, encryption, and role-based access control.
How do I upgrade my subscription?
If you need to upgrade your subscription to the next Plan, or need a custom Plan you can contact our team to discuss and activate.
What if my question isn’t answered here, who can I ask?
If you have additional questions please contact info@nats.io.
What are your payment schedule and terms?
The initial payment is pro-rated for that month based on the day of the month you sign up. Subsequent monthly payments will be billed to the credit card on file.
