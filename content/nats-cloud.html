NATS Cloud

NATS Cloud is a managed service based on NATS, allowing you to instantly connect your resources to NATS messaging and scale without the need to maintain or provision any hardware yourself.

FAQ

If I am using NATS already on premise, why should I switch to NATS Cloud?
NATS Cloud is hosted by the industry leader Amazon, and managed by the NATS team. You get a scalable and automated NATS cluster maintained by the best messaging team in the industry, without having to manage anything yourself. You’ll automatically receive the latest updates and get support as part of your subscription too.
Can I try NATS Cloud for free?
We maintain a demo server @ telnet demo.nats.io 4222 but for ongoing use and access to NATS Cloud, you must sign up for a plan.
What is your Service Level Agreement
Because NATS Cloud runs on an isolated Amazon Web Services instance, our SLA is the same as AWS’.
